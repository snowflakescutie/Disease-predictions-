Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What is Disease Prediction From Symptoms?

A: Disease Prediction From Symptoms is a system that predicts possible diseases based on symptoms provided by the user.

Q: How accurate is the prediction?

A: The accuracy of the prediction depends on various factors, including the quality and quantity of data used for training the prediction model.

Q: Can I trust the predictions?

A: While the prediction model is based on machine learning algorithms, it is important to consult a healthcare professional for accurate diagnosis and treatment.

Q: How does the app collect symptom information?

A: The app collects symptom information through user input. Users are prompted to input their symptoms, which are then used to generate predictions.

Q: Is my personal information secure?

A: Yes, we take privacy and data security seriously. Your personal information and symptom data are encrypted and stored securely. We adhere to strict privacy policies to ensure the confidentiality of your information.

Q: Can I use the app for emergency situations?

A: No, the app is not intended for emergency situations. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please seek immediate medical attention by calling emergency services or visiting the nearest emergency room.

Q: Is the app suitable for children?

A:The app is designed for users of all ages, including children. However, parents or guardians should supervise children's use of the app, and it should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice or diagnosis.
